ingredients

  • a bag of cooked chicken

    • a little vague, i know. ill explain in a bit.

  • 2 cups mayo
  • 1/2 cup plain greek yogurt (plain)
  • 1/2 cup sour cream
  • 1 tablespoon ranch dressing powder
  • 2 apples (red, sweet)
  • 1 cup grapes
  • 1/2 cup celery greens & tops
  • 1/2 cup walnuts

steps

  1. mix the mayo, yogurt, and sour cream
  2. add the ranch dressing powder and then mix well
  3. finely chop the celery greens and tops
  4. chop walnuts into small pieces
  5. cut grapes in half
  6. add and mix celery, walnuts, and grapes
  7. chop apples into bite size cubes--mix immediately to prevent oxidation
